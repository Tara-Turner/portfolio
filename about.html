Internship Experience

The Internship through "Maxx Power" has given me an opportunity to explore a number of skills in the CS industry. I was tasked with not only building this portfolio, but handling data, building a website, and creating policies as well as terms and conditions.


Data Management

My first week of the internship, I was assigned to the Business Intellegence and Data Governance team. My team worked together to organize and analyze data from 2010-2019 into their own categories based on the reason for the power outage. We also put this data into several graphs to show the amount of outages per month and the percentage of each outage reason.


Website Development

My second week of the internship, I was assigned to the Web Development team. My team decided it would be much easier to build our own website from scratch rather than fix the site provided to us. We worked with the data and security teams to gather some information about the privacy policy, terms and conditions, and the outage charts to display on our website. We ran into a few issues with the formatting of the website, but we came together to figure out a way to overcome our challenges.


Cyber Security

For my final week of the internship, I was placed in the Security group. Here I wrote a few differnt policies and generated the terms and conditions for the Website Development team to encorporate in their website. At first, it was sdifficult to figure out how to write a policy, but after coming up with a basic outline, my team and I did a lot of research and was able to easily come up with some policies for "MAXX Power".
